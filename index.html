Hello world!

I’m an active researcher specializing in molecular evolution. My research explores the evolutionary dynamics of genes, with a strong focus on computational molecular evolution. I am a firm believer in reproducible open science and supporter of Open Science movement. I make my data and code accessible through GitHub for the scientific community. As an Editorial Board Member at PLOS ONE and Discover Applied Sciences, I actively contribute to advancing open access scientific publishing. I’m also a member of the Open Bioinformatics Foundation (OBF). If you're just starting out with coding, check out my book, Basic Python Programming for Biologists. Critically check my work and join the Open Science movement in pushing the boundaries of free knowledge!
